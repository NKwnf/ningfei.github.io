About Me

      I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Department of Computer Science at University of California, Irvine advised by Prof. Qi Alfred Chen.

      My research interests are autonomous Cyber-Physical Systems security and Machine Learning security, especially in the context of Autonomous Driving systems.

      Before joining UCI, I received my M.S. and B.E. degree from Lehigh University and Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications.
